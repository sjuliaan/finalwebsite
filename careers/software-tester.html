Career Overview

Software testers play a critical role in the software development lifecycle, ensuring that applications meet quality standards and function as intended.

Software testers are responsible for identifying bugs and issues in software applications before they are released to users. They work closely with developers and other stakeholders to ensure that software products are reliable, efficient, and user-friendly.

Testers use a variety of testing methods, including manual testing, automated testing, and performance testing, to evaluate software applications. They also document test results and provide feedback to development teams to improve software quality.

With the rise of agile methodologies and continuous integration, software testing has become an integral part of the development process, ensuring that applications are delivered with high quality and reliability.
