Career Overview

Artificial Intelligence professionals develop systems that can perform tasks that typically require human intelligence, such as visual perception, speech recognition, decision-making, and language translation.

United States Market

In the US, AI roles are experiencing explosive growth with approximately 40,000 openings projected each year. The demand is particularly high in tech hubs like Silicon Valley, Seattle, and Boston, where major tech companies and startups are investing heavily in AI research and applications.

Philippines Market

In the Philippines, AI careers are growing rapidly as the country positions itself as an emerging tech hub in Southeast Asia. Major demand comes from Manila, Cebu, and Clark, with multinational companies establishing AI research centers and development teams to leverage local talent at competitive costs.

AI professionals in both countries need to continuously update their skills as the field evolves rapidly. The role requires a blend of technical expertise in machine learning, deep learning, and neural networks, along with domain knowledge to apply AI solutions to specific industry problems.
