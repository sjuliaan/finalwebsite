Career Overview

Computer Network Architects design and build data communication networks that are the backbone of modern business operations.

United States Market

In the US, Network Architects are in steady demand with about 10,500 openings projected each year. The role is particularly strong in technology hubs like Silicon Valley, Seattle, and Austin, where cloud computing and digital transformation are driving network infrastructure needs.

Philippines Market

In the Philippines, the role is growing faster than the US average due to increasing digitalization of businesses and the expansion of BPO and IT services sectors. Major demand comes from Manila, Cebu, and Clark, with many multinational companies establishing network operations centers in the country.

Network Architects in both countries need to stay current with emerging technologies like SD-WAN, 5G networks, and cloud networking solutions. The role requires balancing technical expertise with business acumen to design networks that support organizational goals.
