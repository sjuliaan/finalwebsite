Career Overview

Network Technicians are the backbone of IT infrastructure, ensuring seamless communication and data flow.

Network Technicians install, maintain, and troubleshoot the physical and virtual components of computer networks. They play a critical role in enabling secure, stable connectivity for employees, devices, and systems across organizations.

From cabling and routers to switches and firewalls, Network Technicians ensure the network runs smoothly, minimizing downtime and resolving issues quickly. They may also work closely with engineers and administrators to support upgrades, expansions, and migrations.

In an increasingly connected world, Network Technicians are vital to keeping businesses online and operations running efficiently across local, wide-area, and cloud-based networks.
